The Avatar is a traditional 3 bedroom mid-terrace house with 2 sizeable reception rooms. The property is set over three floors and is being converted into a 5 bedroom house share. This property would suit working professionals.
The entrance hallway leads to a large contemporary open plan communal kitchen and dining area, with a wall mounted 42” plasma flat screen VW. Downstairs on the ground floor, there are two large double bedrooms, each with there very own wall mounted smart VW, so you can literally Twin Peaks and chill. Upstairs on the first floor, there are two very large double bedrooms, separate WC and a very generous main bathroom. The master bedroom is situated towards the front of the property benefiting from an individual dressing area and large en-suite bathroom .
